The cousins tore through Grandma's backyard in record time this year — all the eggs were found in under ten minutes. Grandpa hid a few extra-tricky ones in the flower beds that took a bit longer to track down.

## Highlights

- Dinner was ham, scalloped potatoes, and Grandma's famous pie
- The little ones got matching Easter baskets from Grandma
- We took the annual group photo on the porch steps

Already looking forward to next year.
