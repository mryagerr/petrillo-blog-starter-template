We're heading up to the lake house for the long weekend. Plan to arrive Friday evening; checkout is Monday by noon.

## The plan

- Friday: arrive, settle in, cookout on the dock
- Saturday: boating and swimming all day, campfire at night
- Sunday: lazy morning, hike to the overlook in the afternoon
- Monday: pack up and head home

## Packing list

- Swimsuits and towels
- Bug spray (it gets bad near the water at dusk)
- Life jackets for the little ones
- Something for the campfire — cards, s'mores supplies, whatever

Let the group chat know if you need a ride or have room for extra passengers.
