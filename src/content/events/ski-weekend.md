First family ski trip in a few years, and it did not disappoint. We rented the big cabin at Snowbound Lodge and split into groups based on skill level — the beginners stuck to the bunny hill while the rest of us tackled the blues.

## Highlights

- Nobody got hurt, which felt like a win given how many of us hadn't skied in years
- The lodge's fireplace room became home base for hot cocoa and cards each night
- Cousin Mike lost the annual card tournament and has to plan next year's trip

## Notes for next time

- Book the lodge earlier — we almost lost our reservation
- Bring extra gloves, everyone's got wet within an hour
