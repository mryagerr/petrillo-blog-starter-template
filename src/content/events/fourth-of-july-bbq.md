A great turnout this year — over 20 of us packed into Aunt Rosa's backyard for burgers, corn on the cob, and way too much watermelon.

## How it went

- Uncle Tony's ribs were the star of the grill again
- The kids ran through the sprinkler most of the afternoon
- We walked down to the lake around 9pm and caught the fireworks from the dock — best seats in town

## For next time

- Bring more chairs — we ran out by early evening
- The mosquitoes get bad after sunset, so pack repellent
