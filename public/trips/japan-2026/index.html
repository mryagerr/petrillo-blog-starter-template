Japan, June 2026

June 5–24, 2026 · 19 nights · 4 cities · 0 cars

Overview

Nineteen nights, four legs, and not a single rental car — just Shinkansen, local rail, buses, and a lot of walking. Michael, Brittany, and their two boys (7 and 4) made their way from Tokyo Bay to Hakone to Osaka and back to Tokyo, forwarding luggage ahead with Yamato at every stop so the only thing anyone carried between hotels was a day bag. This page is the retrospective — the route, the highlights, and the photos.
