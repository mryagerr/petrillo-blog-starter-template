Practical Info

Shuttle basics

Zion Canyon Scenic Drive is shuttle-only during the regular season (roughly March–November). Shuttles run frequently from the Zion Canyon Visitor Center to Temple of Sinawava (9 stops). The free Springdale town shuttle connects town to the pedestrian entrance — no need to drive into the park at all.

Entrance fee

$35 per vehicle for a 7-day pass, or use an America the Beautiful annual pass ($80) for free entry.

June weather

Hot — canyon highs often 95–105°F with lows near 65–70°F. Hike early (on the trail by 7am), carry 3+ liters of water per person, and watch for afternoon monsoon storms (flash-flood risk in narrow canyons).
